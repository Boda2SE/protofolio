- Gym Graduation Project

descriprion

This project is a Gym Management Application developed as a part of a university graduation project. It allows users to manage their memberships and reserve gym equipment, while also providing administrators with the ability to manage facilities and members efficiently. The application was developed by university students, and I personally worked on around 75% of the total system.

detail

  • Managing memberships and subscriptions.
  • Managing gym equipment and facilities.
  • Ability to reserve tools and equipment through the app.
  • Managing gym schedules and activities.
