Material App - Assiut University Faculty of Computers and AI

Description

This project is a Material App developed as part of the graduation project for the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence, Assiut University. The application provides educational material that works offline, enabling students to access lectures, notes, and assignments without the need for an internet connection. The app focuses on enhancing the learning experience for students by offering easily accessible and organized study materials, ensuring that students can study anytime, anywhere. I worked on developing the app's core features and functionalities, creating a seamless offline experience.

Project Features

  • Offline access to educational materials including lectures, notes, and assignments.
  • Organized and categorized study resources for easy navigation.
  • Simple and user-friendly interface for students to access content.
  • Support for various subjects and courses related to the Faculty of Computers and AI.
  • Search functionality to quickly find specific materials.
  • Regular updates for new content to be added to the app.
